About Us

Hello! We are a team of designers from Olin College of Engineering. We worked on this project as our Senior Capstone in Engineering. We conducted human-centered design research to identify high-impact web accessibility problems for people who are blind, and design novel solutions that utilize machine learning.​ Next, UT Austin's Professor Danna Gurari will create a dataset, or multiple, to help developers implement the novel solutions we have identified.​ This 9-month project was funded by a Microsoft AI for Accessibility (AI4A) grant.
